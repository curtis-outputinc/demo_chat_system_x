# Availability and how to reach a broker

The prequalifier is available on the broker's website 24/7. Real-time
replies from a licensed broker happen during normal business hours.
Consultations booked outside those hours are confirmed the next business
day.

The specific broker's office hours, locations, and direct contact methods
are confirmed during the first call.

## Booking

Visitors can book a free initial consultation through the booking link the
prequalifier provides. If a visitor prefers to be contacted, the
prequalifier captures first name and a phone number at the end of the
interview and hands the file to the broker for a callback.
