# About the prequalifier and what it covers

Mortgage Prequalifier Demo is an intelligent prequalification system built for
licensed mortgage brokers. It runs a borrower through a structured
prequalification interview, captures the file, and hands a clean summary
to the broker so the broker only spends time on files worth their time.
It does not give specific rate quotes, qualifying decisions, lender
recommendations, or tax, legal, or investment advice.

## Lending scope

This prequalifier is built for brokers who handle the full lending
spectrum:

- Conventional A-lender mortgages: prime credit, verifiable income, mainstream
  properties, insured and uninsured.
- Alternative mortgages: B-lender, MIC, and trust-company files for borrowers
  with bruised credit, self-employed and stated-income scenarios, non-prime
  properties, recent credit events, or shorter income history.
- Private mortgages: equity-based files, short-term bridge or rescue
  financing, complex properties, urgent closes, and second or third positions.

A borrower with bruised credit, irregular income, a non-traditional property,
or a tight timeline is a core case here, not an edge case.

## Mortgage types handled

First-time purchases, move-up purchases, refinances, renewals, switches,
equity takeouts, second mortgages, private seconds and thirds, bridge
financing, self-employed and stated-income scenarios, new-immigrant and
new-to-credit files, investment property financing, rural and acreage
properties, mixed-use, multi-unit, and case-by-case construction.

## What the prequalifier does

- Runs the borrower through a structured interview at their pace, one
  question at a time, with reassurance and plain language.
- Reads which lending bucket (conventional, alternative, or private) the
  file most likely lands in, without making any approval promises.
- Captures the file shape (goal, property, income, credit range, debts,
  urgency, anything unusual) and hands it to the broker as a clean
  synopsis.
- Collects only what the broker needs to make contact: first name and a
  phone number. No identification, no documents, no full SSN or SIN, no
  account numbers.

## What the prequalifier does not do

- It does not tell a borrower they are approved, declined, or likely to
  qualify for a specific amount or product.
- It does not quote rates, payments, or maximum borrowing amounts.
- It does not name a specific lender as the borrower's best fit.
- It does not collect sensitive documents in chat. Those move through the
  broker's secure workflow after the first call.

## Service area

The brokers who use this prequalifier are licensed across the United
States and Canada. There is no single default market. Specific lender
availability, program eligibility, and regulatory rules vary by state,
province, and territory; the local broker confirms what applies.

## Consultations

Brokers using this prequalifier offer free initial consultations. A
visitor can request one through the prequalification flow or by booking a
call directly through the booking link.
