# Pricing, estimates, and financing

## Standard service rate

Our standard hourly rate is **$120 per hour** during regular
business hours (Monday to Friday 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Saturday
9:00 AM to 3:00 PM). Most residential repairs are one to three
hours plus parts.

## After-hours and emergency rate

Calls dispatched outside regular business hours (evenings past
6:00 PM, Sundays, and holidays) run at our **after-hours emergency
rate of $180 per hour**, with a 1-hour minimum. This is separate
from and in addition to any diagnostic fee.

## Diagnostic fee

For a repair service call, we charge a diagnostic fee to come out,
inspect the system, identify the fault, and give an accurate
repair quote. The fee is applied toward the cost of the repair if
you approve the work on the same visit. Diagnostic pricing is
confirmed on the phone when scheduling.

## Free estimates on replacements and new installs

Quotes for a new furnace, new AC, new heat pump, or full-system
replacement are always free. A technician (or a comfort advisor for
larger residential jobs, or a commercial account manager for
commercial jobs) comes to the site, takes measurements, and puts
together a written proposal.

## Why we cannot quote an exact price over the phone or in this chat

Real numbers depend on the equipment, the access, the ductwork, the
electrical, the permit, and what we find when we actually look at
your system. What we can say for free: whether the work is
something we do, what a range typically looks like, what factors
drive the price up or down, and how the estimate process works. For
an actual number, we book a visit.

## What a written quote includes

Every quote includes: the exact equipment (make, model, size,
efficiency), the labor, any duct or venting work, thermostat, the
electrical, disposal of old equipment, permits, and the warranty
terms. If it is not on the quote, it is not in the price.

## Financing

We offer financing on approved credit for larger repairs, new
installs, and replacement systems. Options typically include:

- 0% promotional financing for qualifying customers over 12 to 18
  months.
- Longer-term financing (up to 10 years) at competitive rates.
- Deferred-interest periods on some promotions.

Financing is coordinated at the time the quote is signed. Actual
terms depend on the lender, the customer's credit, and the
project size.

## Federal, state, and utility rebates

There are three flavors of incentives you may be able to stack on
an efficiency upgrade:

- **Federal**: the federal 25C Energy Efficient Home Improvement
  tax credit expired at the end of 2025. If any successor credits
  come out, we let clients know at the time of the quote.
- **State and local**: many states run programs for high-efficiency
  heat pumps, insulation, and electrification. We check the current
  state programs when we prepare a quote.
- **Utility**: many electric and gas utilities offer cash rebates
  for high-efficiency AC, heat pumps, smart thermostats, and demand-
  response participation. We help clients apply for whatever their
  utility is running at the time.

We do not quote a specific rebate amount in the chat because
programs change and eligibility depends on your utility, your
county, and the equipment. On the quote visit we look up the
current programs and factor them in.

## Warranty coverage and cost

New equipment comes with a manufacturer parts warranty (typically
5 to 10 years on residential furnaces, ACs, and heat pumps; often
1 year on commercial equipment, with extended options). We include
a 1-year labor warranty on installations by default. Extended labor
warranties are available at purchase; they cover the technician's
time on a warranty repair for the extended period.

## What we do NOT do on pricing

- We do not quote a specific price over the phone or in this chat
  without seeing the equipment. We can quote pricing style (hourly,
  diagnostic fee, free estimate on replacement) but not the number
  for a specific job.
- We do not sell equipment a customer does not need. If a repair
  makes sense and the system has good life left, we say so.
- We do not lock a customer out of a competing quote. If you want
  to compare, we encourage it and stand by our numbers.
