# Contact and hours

Callers who want to reach ABC Comfort Heating and Air Conditioning have three options: phone,
email, or booking a service call directly through this chat.

## Phone

Our main line is **(123) 555-5555**. This number answers 24 hours
a day, 7 days a week for emergency service. During regular
business hours it is also the number for scheduling routine
service calls, quotes, and general questions.

## Email

**info@abccomfort.com** reaches our office. We reply the same day
during business hours and the next business day if you write us
in the evening or on the weekend. For an active emergency, call
the phone line; email is not the fastest path.

## Regular business hours (office and standard-rate service)

- Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM
- Sunday: closed for standard service (emergency line still on)

Standard-rate service calls are dispatched during regular business
hours. Anything requested outside those windows falls under our
after-hours emergency rate. See `pricing-and-financing.md` for the
rate structure.

## 24/7 emergency service

We answer the phone at any hour for HVAC emergencies. What counts
as an emergency and what to do while waiting for a tech is in
`emergency-service.md`.

## Booking a service call from this chat

The chat can capture your name, phone number, best time of day
for a callback, and a short description of what is going on with
your system. Our team follows up during business hours (or right
away if the situation is urgent).

## Response times we aim for

- Emergency dispatch: same day, most cases within a few hours
  depending on time of day and how loaded the schedule is.
- Standard service call: usually next business day for
  non-emergency issues.
- Estimate for a replacement or new install: usually within 2 to
  3 business days.

We confirm exact timing on the call. We do not promise a
specific arrival minute in the chat because dispatch depends on
what the tech ahead of you finds on site.
