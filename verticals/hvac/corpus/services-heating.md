# Heating services

ABC Comfort Heating and Air Conditioning repairs, maintains, and installs the full range of
residential and commercial heating equipment. Below is what we
handle, in plain language.

## Gas furnaces

We service and install natural-gas and propane furnaces in every
efficiency tier: single-stage (100% on or off), two-stage (high
and low), and variable-speed / modulating (adjusts continuously
for the smoothest and quietest airflow). We work on high-efficiency
condensing furnaces (typically 90 to 98% AFUE, vented in plastic
piping) and on older mid-efficiency units that vent through a
traditional metal flue.

Common furnace calls we handle:

- Furnace will not start or blowing cold air (dirty flame sensor,
  failed igniter, tripped safety switch, closed gas valve, bad
  thermostat).
- Loud noises: banging on ignition (delayed ignition, burner
  issue), squealing (blower bearings or belt), rattling (loose
  panel or blower wheel).
- Short cycling (overheating from restricted airflow, thermostat
  trouble, or oversized furnace).
- Cracked heat exchanger (serious safety issue; carbon monoxide
  risk).
- Water pooling near a condensing furnace (clogged condensate
  drain).
- Full furnace replacement, including venting, gas line
  adjustments, and load-calc-based sizing.

## Electric furnaces

Full service on electric-resistance furnaces, including sequencer
issues, heating element replacement, blower repair, and thermostat
integration. Common in homes without gas service.

## Boilers

We service and install natural gas, propane, and oil boilers
feeding hot water baseboards, radiators, or radiant in-floor
loops. Both cast-iron traditional boilers and modern high-efficiency
condensing boilers. Common calls: no heat, low pressure, banging
or gurgling (air in the system), stuck circulator pump, failed
zone valve, cracked expansion tank, ignition failure.

## Heat pumps (air-source)

We install and service both standard and cold-climate heat pumps.
A heat pump moves heat instead of generating it, so it provides
heating in winter and cooling in summer from the same equipment.
Cold-climate models keep heating efficiently well below freezing.
We handle single-stage, two-stage, and variable-speed / inverter
heat pumps, and we set up hybrid (dual-fuel) systems where an
electric heat pump handles mild weather and a gas furnace kicks in
during extreme cold.

Common heat pump calls: lukewarm air (normal for a heat pump; not
a fault), heavy ice or a stuck defrost cycle, auxiliary heat
running when it should not, no cooling in summer, no heating in
winter, refrigerant issues, thermostat setup for heat pump versus
furnace logic.

## Ductless heat pumps and mini-splits

Single-zone and multi-zone ductless systems that heat and cool
without ductwork. Great for additions, basements, garages, older
homes without existing ducts, or rooms that were always too hot or
too cold.

## Radiant and in-floor heating

Hydronic in-floor loops (heated water pumped through tubing under
the floor) and electric radiant panels. Service includes zone
valves, manifolds, mixing valves, thermostats, and troubleshooting
uneven heat.

## Unit heaters

For warehouses, garages, shops, and light industrial spaces. Gas
or electric, suspended-ceiling mounted, with tube exhaust or
propeller fans.

## Fuel type conversions

We convert homes and businesses from oil to natural gas or
propane, from gas to a heat pump (electrification), or the reverse
if a client needs to keep a gas backup. Conversion involves the
new equipment, venting adjustments, fuel line work, and updated
thermostat logic.

## What we do not touch

- Wood stoves and pellet stoves.
- Fireplaces (gas fireplace inserts are handled by a fireplace
  specialist).
- Standalone space heaters and infrared patio heaters (we install
  built-in overhead radiant heaters for commercial spaces, but
  not portable consumer units).
