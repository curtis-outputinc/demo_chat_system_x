# Warranties and guarantees

## Manufacturer parts warranty (on new equipment)

Most major HVAC manufacturers offer 5 to 10 years on parts for
residential furnaces, ACs, and heat pumps. Some brands go longer
on the compressor or heat exchanger specifically. Commercial
equipment is more variable: standard is often 1 year on parts with
optional extended terms.

Registration matters. If a new residential system is not registered
with the manufacturer within 60 to 90 days of installation, the
parts warranty period usually gets cut roughly in half. We register
every system we install on the customer's behalf; the customer
gets a copy of the registration confirmation.

## ABC HVAC labor warranty (on new installs)

Every new equipment installation comes with a 1-year labor
warranty by default. If something we installed fails during that
first year, our labor to fix it is covered.

Extended labor warranties are available at the time of purchase
for 5 or 10 years. These cover the technician's time on any
warranty repair through the extended period. Recommended for
customers who want fully budgeted comfort.

## ABC HVAC labor warranty (on repairs)

Every repair is warrantied for 90 days on the same fault. If the
part we replaced fails again on the same issue within 90 days, we
come back and make it right at no additional labor cost. Parts
inside their manufacturer warranty are also covered for the
manufacturer's period.

## What voids the warranty

- Modifications or repairs by an unlicensed contractor or a
  homeowner attempting DIY repair.
- Failure to perform recommended annual maintenance (this varies
  by manufacturer; some require documented maintenance to keep
  the parts warranty active).
- Damage from flooding, lightning, freezing, or other events not
  caused by the equipment itself.
- Refrigerant charge changes done by an unlicensed technician (this
  is federally regulated).

## Comfort guarantee on new installs

If a new system does not deliver the comfort we promised in the
quote (right temperature in every conditioned room, right
humidity range, right airflow), we come back and rebalance,
readjust, or make it right. We stand behind the design as much as
the equipment.

## Satisfaction guarantee on repair work

If a repair does not fix what it was supposed to fix, we do not
charge for the follow-up diagnostic. We come back, diagnose again,
and reset the scope of work with the customer before doing more.

## What is NOT covered

- Consumables like filters, batteries, and belts (belts are
  inspected under maintenance plans).
- Damage from outside events: freezing pipes, lightning, floods,
  storms, rodent damage.
- Systems installed by another contractor: we service any brand,
  but our labor warranty applies to work we performed.

## How to file a warranty claim

Call the office line or email info@abchvac.com. Give us the address
and a description of the problem. If the equipment is under our
labor warranty, we schedule the repair at no labor cost. If it is
under manufacturer parts warranty only, we handle the paperwork
with the manufacturer and bill only the labor portion. If we
installed the system and registered it, you do not need to know
where the paperwork is; we have it on file.
