# Maintenance plans

Preventive maintenance is the single most cost-effective thing a
homeowner or business can do for their HVAC system. It keeps
manufacturer warranties valid, catches worn parts before they
break, drops energy bills, and extends equipment life. We offer
three residential tiers and three commercial tiers.

## Residential plans

### Silver plan

- One annual visit (typically before heating season).
- Full inspection: heat exchanger, burners, blower, gas valve,
  ignition, safety controls, thermostat, condensate drain, and
  filter check.
- Standard 1-inch filter change (if you supply the filter).
- 10% discount on any parts or labor during that visit.

Best for: homeowners on a budget who want minimum coverage.

### Gold plan

- Two annual visits (one heating season, one cooling season).
- Everything in Silver.
- Coil cleaning on the AC visit.
- 15% discount on any repairs.
- Priority scheduling ahead of non-plan customers.

Best for: most homeowners. This is the plan the majority pick.

### Platinum plan

- Two annual visits.
- Everything in Gold.
- Free standard filters throughout the year (up to four included
  filters per year).
- 20% discount on any repairs.
- No overtime charge for emergency calls after hours.
- Waived diagnostic fee if we come out for a repair.

Best for: homeowners with older systems, systems under warranty
they want to protect, or anyone who wants peace of mind.

## Commercial plans

Commercial equipment runs harder than residential and needs more
frequent attention. We size the plan to your equipment and your
industry:

### Quarterly plan (four visits / year)

- One visit per season.
- Full inspection: coils, filters, belts, electrical, refrigerant,
  drainage, safety, and controls.
- Written service report with any recommended repairs.
- Priority scheduling and reduced hourly rate on repairs.

Best for: offices, retail, professional service spaces, light
commercial.

### Bi-monthly plan (six visits / year)

- Six visits, evenly spaced.
- Everything in the Quarterly plan, more often.
- Filter changes included on every visit.

Best for: restaurants, medical and dental clinics, gyms, buildings
with heavy usage.

### Monthly plan (twelve visits / year)

- Twelve visits.
- Everything above, plus monthly walk-through and full electrical
  test.
- Priority emergency dispatch, no after-hours rate.

Best for: hospitals, data centers, cleanrooms, manufacturing,
apartment complexes, and any building where downtime is expensive.

## What every plan includes

- A dedicated service log tied to your equipment (make, model,
  serial number, install date, service history).
- Reminders when the next visit is due.
- Written service report after each visit with any recommendations.
- Manufacturer warranty registration and paperwork on any new
  equipment installed on plan.

## What is NOT included

- Replacement parts and materials beyond filters. Repairs are
  billed at the plan-discounted rate.
- Major repairs like compressor replacements, motor replacements,
  or coil replacements.
- Full system replacements.

## Cancellation

Plans are annual. You can cancel at any time; there is no
long-term lock-in. If we have already made the visits and applied
the discount, we prorate the remainder.

## Do maintenance plans actually save money

Yes. Two things drive the savings: (1) small issues get fixed
before they become large ones, and (2) a clean, tuned system uses
less energy. Real-world savings vary by home, climate, and
equipment condition, but most customers cover the plan cost
within the year and see it clearly in years two and three.
