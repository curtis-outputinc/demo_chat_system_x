# Cooling services

ABC Comfort Heating and Air Conditioning repairs, maintains, and installs air conditioning and
cooling equipment for homes and businesses. What we handle:

## Central air conditioners

Traditional split-system central AC: an outdoor condenser paired
with an indoor evaporator coil sitting on top of your furnace or
air handler. Every efficiency tier from single-stage to two-stage
to variable-speed / inverter-driven. Both traditional R-410A
systems (existing equipment) and new low-GWP A2L systems (R-32,
R-454B) for new installs going forward.

Common central AC calls we handle:

- AC runs but blows warm air (dirty filter, blocked condenser,
  tripped breaker, refrigerant leak, failed compressor).
- Frozen evaporator coil (restricted airflow, low refrigerant,
  running the AC when outdoor temperature is too low).
- Water leaking indoors from the air handler (clogged condensate
  drain line, cracked drain pan, frozen coil that just started
  to melt).
- Short cycling (oversized system, thermostat problem, dirty
  filter, low refrigerant, failing compressor).
- Loud noises (bad capacitor, failing fan motor, worn compressor
  bearings, loose panel).
- Musty smell from vents (bacterial or mold growth on the wet
  evaporator coil, often called dirty sock syndrome).
- Weak airflow (dirty filter, disconnected duct, failing blower).
- Complete AC replacement with load-calc-based sizing.

## Ductless mini-splits

Single-zone and multi-zone ductless AC / heat pumps. One outdoor
compressor drives one or more low-profile indoor wall or ceiling
cassettes. Great for spaces without ducts, additions, offices in a
basement, garages, or any room that is always the wrong
temperature. Whisper-quiet and efficient.

## Heat pumps in cooling mode

Same equipment as our heat pump heating offering; in summer a heat
pump acts like a normal AC. See `services-heating.md` for the heat
pump detail.

## Window units and portable units

We install and service window air conditioners and portable units
for spot cooling. We do not typically stock them (customer
supplies the unit) but we install, secure, and start them up.

## Commercial cooling (see services-commercial.md)

Rooftop package units, split systems, VRF, chillers, cooling
towers, precision cooling for server rooms, and dedicated outdoor
air systems.

## What we do about refrigerant

Under normal operation, an AC never needs refrigerant added. It is
a sealed system. If a system is low, there is a leak. We find the
leak, repair it, and recharge the system correctly. We do not top
off leaking systems and send you on your way. Because environmental
regulations phased out high-GWP refrigerants like R-410A, newer
installations use A2L refrigerants (R-32, R-454B) which require
specifically designed equipment. Older R-410A systems can still be
serviced with reclaimed refrigerant; supply is finite and pricing
will trend up over time.

## Sizing new AC installs

Every new AC install starts with a Manual J load calculation. That
looks at square footage, window orientation, insulation, air
sealing, local climate, ductwork condition, and internal heat
loads. A bigger AC is not automatically a better AC: an oversized
AC short-cycles, does not remove humidity properly, cools unevenly,
and wears out faster. Right-sizing is more comfortable and cheaper
to run.
