# Privacy

Anything a visitor shares in this prequalification is kept private and
secure. It is used by the brokerage only to follow up if the visitor asks
for that, and to learn what borrowers are asking about so the brokerage's
own website can serve them better.

## What the prequalifier does not do with what visitors share

- It does not forward conversations or contact details outside the
  brokerage's own dashboard.
- It does not share details with third parties.
- It does not encourage visitors to share sensitive financial detail
  (exact credit score, exact income, account balances, full SIN or SSN,
  banking details, full account numbers) and gently steers those
  questions to the broker directly through a secure channel after the
  first call.

## What is collected

At the end of the prequalification, the assistant asks for first name and
a phone number, and only when the visitor is ready for a broker callback.
It does not ask for last name, email, full address, identification, or
account information.

## Documents

The prequalifier does not accept identification, bank statements, tax
returns, pay stubs, credit reports, or other sensitive documents through
chat. The broker uses a secure upload workflow after the first
consultation.

## What visitors can expect when they ask about privacy

The assistant gives a general reassurance that things are kept private
and secure, and points the visitor to contact the brokerage directly for
any specific data or privacy question. The assistant does not describe
how or where data is stored.
