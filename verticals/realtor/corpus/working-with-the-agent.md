# Working with the brokerage

**Q: Are you a real person or an AI assistant?**
This is an intelligent website chat system. It answers common questions, collects details, and connects visitors with the real agent when needed.

**Q: How much does it cost to use the brokerage as a buyer?**
Commission structures vary by market and have shifted recently. The agent walks through the specifics during the first conversation.

**Q: How do I contact a live agent directly?**
You can ask in this chat to be connected, or use the booking link to schedule a call. The agent reaches back out as soon as possible.

**Q: What geographical areas does the brokerage service?**
The brokerage serves specific neighbourhoods and surrounding areas; the agent confirms the exact list at the start. For other areas, the agent can usually refer you to a trusted colleague.

**Q: What are the brokerage's office hours?**
Office hours and availability are confirmed during the first conversation. The chat is available 24/7; real-time responses from the agent happen during normal hours.

**Q: Do you handle commercial real estate?**
The agent's primary focus is residential. The agent can refer you to a commercial specialist for office, retail, industrial, or multi-family commercial deals.

**Q: Can I sign up for email alerts for new listings?**
Yes. Share your ideal budget, neighbourhood, and bedroom count along with your email and the agent can set up automatic alerts.

**Q: Where can I read reviews of the brokerage?**
The brokerage has a testimonials page on the website and verified reviews online. The agent can point you to specific links.

**Q: Can you recommend trusted local professionals?**
Yes. The agent has a curated list of lenders, inspectors, lawyers or settlement professionals, contractors, stagers, photographers, movers, and other vendors.

**Q: What is the difference between a real estate agent and a Realtor?**
All Realtors are licensed real estate professionals, but they are also members of a professional association with its own code of ethics and standards (in Canada and the U.S., the relevant body and trademark differ; the agent can confirm locally).

**Q: Can I text the agent directly?**
Yes. Many agents communicate by text for quick updates and showing coordination. Confirm preferred method during your first call.

**Q: Can the brokerage help me sell vacant land?**
Yes. The agent helps price, market, and sell vacant land and acreage and can review zoning and utility access with you or refer to a specialist.

**Q: How do I unsubscribe from property alerts?**
Click the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any alert email or let the agent know to remove you from the list.

**Q: What languages does the team speak?**
That depends on the team. The agent confirms which languages are supported.

**Q: How often will the agent communicate with me?**
Communication frequency is tailored to your preference: daily updates, weekly check-ins, or as-needed when there are real updates. The agent confirms your preferred method (call, text, email).

**Q: What makes this brokerage different?**
The brokerage combines responsive service with local market knowledge and a focus on stress-free transactions. The agent can walk through specifics during the first call.

**Q: Can the brokerage help with corporate relocation?**
Yes. The brokerage offers relocation support including neighbourhood orientation, virtual tours, school references, and temporary housing referrals.

**Q: Can you calculate my exact monthly mortgage payment?**
Only a rough estimate can be given without your specific credit profile, taxes, and insurance. A licensed mortgage professional can give an exact number. The agent can refer.

**Q: Do you have information on new construction communities?**
Yes. The agent often has relationships with local builders and can share upcoming releases, floor plans, and developer incentives that are not always on the MLS.

**Q: How do I send signed documents to the agent?**
The brokerage uses secure e-signing platforms. You can sign from your phone or computer without printing or scanning.

**Q: Is the information I provide here private?**
Yes. Your information is used to respond to your inquiry and connect you with the right person at the brokerage. Specifics are in the website's privacy policy.

**Q: How fast will the agent respond?**
Response times vary based on time of day and the agent's schedule, with the goal of responding as quickly as possible. Active showings, offer deadlines, and time-sensitive matters are prioritized.

**Q: What information should I provide so the agent can help me best?**
Useful details: name, phone, email, your goal (buy, sell, rent, invest), property address if relevant, budget, preferred area, and timeline. The more context, the better the first reply.

**Q: What is the very next step to begin working together?**
Share your name, contact information, and a brief note about your goals here in the chat, or book a call directly. The agent reaches out from there.

**Q: Can the agent help me buy and sell at the same time?**
Yes. The agent coordinates timelines, pricing, offers, conditions, and any bridge financing conversation that helps the moves line up.

**Q: Can the agent help if I am dealing with an estate or inheriting a property?**
Yes. Estate sales come with extra considerations (executor authority, tax treatment, occupancy, multiple decision-makers). The agent and lawyer help navigate the process.

**Q: Can the agent help with a divorce sale?**
Yes. Divorce or separation sales require sensitivity, clear communication, and careful coordination. The agent works with both sides where appropriate.

**Q: Does the brokerage handle luxury homes?**
Confirm with the agent. Many brokerages have specialists for high-end listings and private off-market opportunities.

**Q: Will the agent help me prepare an offer if I found a home through another source?**
Yes, subject to representation rules in your jurisdiction. The agent walks through how representation works and what the next step looks like.
