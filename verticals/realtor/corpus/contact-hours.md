# Hours and how to reach the brokerage

The assistant is available 24/7 on the brokerage's website. Real-time replies
from an agent happen during normal business hours; bookings made outside those
hours are confirmed the next business day.

The brokerage's specific office hours, locations, and direct contact methods
are confirmed during the first conversation with the agent.

## Booking

Visitors can book a free initial consultation or home evaluation through the
booking link the assistant provides.
