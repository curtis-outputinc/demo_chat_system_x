# Hours and how to reach the practice

The assistant is available 24/7 on the practice's website. Real-time replies
from an advisor happen during normal business hours; consultations booked
outside those hours are confirmed the next business day.

The practice's specific office hours, locations, and direct contact methods
are confirmed during the first conversation with the advisor.

## Booking

Visitors can book a free initial consultation through the booking link the
assistant provides.
