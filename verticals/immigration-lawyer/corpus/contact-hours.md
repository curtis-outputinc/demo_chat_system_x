# Office hours and chat availability

## Office hours

The firm's office is open Monday to Friday, 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM. Calls
during those hours go to the team. Messages left outside those hours are
returned the next business day.

## This chat is always available

This assistant is available around the clock. A visitor can ask general
questions at any hour, including outside office hours and on weekends, and
the assistant will try to help or point them in the right direction.

General questions that do not require legal advice are welcome any time.
Examples include questions about what the firm does, what a consultation
looks like, how a pathway generally works in plain terms, what to expect
from the process, how fees typically work, and how to book a consultation.

For anything that needs legal advice or a review of a specific situation,
a lawyer at the firm handles that during office hours. The assistant offers
to connect the visitor with the firm or share the booking link so they can
set up a free consultation.

## After-hours contact

When a visitor reaches out outside office hours, the assistant still helps
with general questions and captures the visitor's contact details so the
team can follow up the next business day. If the matter is urgent (removal
order, hearing date, permit expiry, procedural fairness letter, refusal
deadline, detention, etc.), see `after-a-refusal-or-notice.md` for how the
assistant handles those situations; urgent leads are flagged for prompt
follow-up regardless of the hour.
